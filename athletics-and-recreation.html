Athletics & Recreation

Now is the right time to be part of Sand Shark fever! In the few short years of USCB athletics, our nine sports teams - which are part of The Sun Conference - have done everything from clinching conference titles to becoming strong competitors in the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA) championships. Whether it's a first start in a college game or playing in front of a Homecoming crowd, traveling as a team to a national tournament or cheering from the stands as your classmate scores the game-winning run, you'll discover a powerful future as a part of Sand Shark nation. www.uscbathletics.com

Living and learning at USCB extends far beyond the classroom. It's about discovering a community and making new friends. It's about jumping in on a game of basketball or going to the NEW SAND SHARK RECREATION CENTER to take a workout class.
